No-nonsense products we're proud of

When it comes to banking, you want an honest deal with no surprises. So we'll always tell you the things you really need to know up front.

  • Current Accounts

    Get more for your money with the M Plus Account. No monthly fee. You'll need to be over 18 and a UK resident to apply online. Check out all the terms, tariff and other important information.

  • Savings Accounts

    Discover a wide world of interest-paying products and watch your cash grow. We're covered by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme. Savings with Virgin Money are covered up to £85,000 per person.

  • Loans

    It's flexible - existing customers can borrow £1,000 - £35,000 and repay over one to five years. Or, if you're borrowing £7,500 or more you may have the option to repay up to seven years.

  • Cards

    Putting all our cards on the table. Whatever credit card you need, we've got a great pack to pick from. Our checker is a quick way to see your chances of being accepted for a card, and it won't affect your credit rating.
